The Frustration Is Real

Cash goes out the door every day. Supplies, deliveries, small expenses, advances. The problem isn't the spending — it's that you have no idea where the money went.

You ask for a receipt and get a shrug. The spreadsheet is two weeks behind. The cash box doesn't match anything.

You're not managing cash — you're guessing.

Why You Can't See Where the Money Goes

  • No one logs transactions in real time — people mean to, but forget. By the end of the day, the details are gone.
  • Receipts disappear — paper receipts get lost, thrown away, or stuffed in a drawer. When you need them, they're nowhere.
  • You rely on trust, not records — and trust works great until it doesn't.
  • You're not on-site — you can't physically watch every cash transaction, so you depend on a system. If that system is a notebook, it fails.

The Fix: 30 Seconds Per Transaction

It's not a system. It's a habit. Log every cash movement the moment it happens. It takes 30 seconds:

  1. Open the app on your phone
  2. Select the cash box
  3. Enter the amount, purpose, and who it went to
  4. A receipt is generated instantly — print, email, or save as PDF

That's it. Do this every time cash moves, and within a week, you'll have a complete picture of where your money is going.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I see where my business cash is going?

Start logging every cash movement — withdrawals, expenses, and returns — with a name, amount, and timestamp. SpendNote does this in about 30 seconds per transaction, so you get a complete picture without creating extra work.

What if I'm not on-site — can I still track cash?

Yes. SpendNote works from any device with a browser. You can check balances, review transactions, and see the full history from your phone — even if the cash is being handled at a different location.

How do I get started without disrupting the current process?

Create a free account, set up a cash box with your current balance, and start logging from your next transaction. There's nothing to install, no training needed. If someone can fill out a form, they can use SpendNote.

Is this accounting software?

No. SpendNote tracks cash movements and generates receipts. It does not do bookkeeping, invoicing, VAT, or tax reporting. Think of it as the paper trail for cash — digital, searchable, and always up to date.
