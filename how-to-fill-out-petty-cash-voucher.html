Traditional Petty Cash Voucher Fields

A standard petty cash voucher requires these fields:

  • Date: When the cash was disbursed
  • Amount: How much cash was given
  • Purpose: What the cash was used for
  • Recipient: Who received the cash
  • Signatures: Both the person giving and receiving the cash must sign

SpendNote receipts include all these fields automatically—you just enter the transaction details and the receipt is generated instantly.

Petty Cash Voucher FAQ

What fields are required on a petty cash voucher?
A petty cash voucher needs: date, amount, purpose/description, recipient name, and signatures from both the person giving and receiving the cash. SpendNote receipts include all these fields automatically.

Can I customize the voucher fields in SpendNote?
Yes. You can customize receipt labels, add your logo, and adjust which fields are visible to match your team's voucher process.

Does SpendNote support multiple currencies?
Yes. Each cash box can use a different currency, and you can customize the currency symbol and format on receipts.
