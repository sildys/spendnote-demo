Our Story

SpendNote started from a simple frustration: tracking petty cash shouldn't be complicated.

We saw teams struggling with spreadsheets, paper receipts getting lost, and managers constantly asking "who took the cash?" There had to be a better way.

"We didn't need accounting software. We just needed to know who had the cash and have proof of handoff."

So we built SpendNote — a focused tool that does one thing well. Create clear, printable receipts for internal cash movements. No invoices, no tax features, no complexity. Just simple proof of handoff.

Today, teams use SpendNote for petty cash, employee advances, event cash, and any situation where internal cash changes hands. It takes 30 seconds to create a receipt, and everyone on the team can see the current balance in real-time.
