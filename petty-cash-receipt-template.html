What is a Petty Cash Receipt Template?

A petty cash receipt template is a standardized form used to document small cash transactions. It typically includes the date, amount, recipient, purpose, and signatures. Common variations include:

  • Petty cash voucher – Used when disbursing cash from a petty cash fund
  • Cash handoff receipt – Documents cash transfers between employees or shifts
  • Internal cash receipt – Records cash received for internal purposes

These templates ensure accountability and provide documentation for accounting and tax purposes.

When Do You Need a Petty Cash Receipt?

Petty cash receipts are essential in several scenarios:

  • Office petty cash – Track small office expenses like supplies, coffee, or parking
  • Event cash handling – Document cash collected or spent at events, fundraisers, or markets
  • Employee cash advances – Record cash given to employees for business expenses
  • Community treasurers – Maintain transparent records for clubs, associations, or non-profits
  • Retail cash floats – Track cash handoffs between shifts or locations

In all these cases, proper documentation protects both the organization and the individuals handling cash.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a petty cash receipt a tax document?

A petty cash receipt is an internal record of a cash transaction. It's not a tax document by itself, but it supports your accounting records. For tax purposes, you typically need the underlying purchase receipt or invoice. SpendNote helps you organize both the cash handoff documentation and any supporting receipts.

Can I print receipts from SpendNote?

Yes. Every receipt in SpendNote can be downloaded as a PDF and printed. You can also email receipts directly to team members or accountants. The digital format means you always have a backup, even if the printed copy is lost.

Can I customize the receipt format?

Yes. SpendNote lets you customize receipt text, labels, and branding. You can adjust the wording to match your organization's terminology or language. This is especially useful for international teams or organizations with specific compliance requirements.
