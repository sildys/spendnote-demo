Quick Questions

Is a petty cash receipt a tax document?

A petty cash receipt is an internal record of a cash transaction. It's not a tax document by itself, but it supports your accounting records. For tax purposes, you typically need the underlying purchase receipt or invoice.

Can I print receipts from SpendNote?

Yes. Every receipt can be downloaded as a PDF and printed. You can also email receipts directly to team members or accountants.

Is SpendNote free?

Yes, SpendNote offers a free plan with 20 transactions per month. Paid plans start at $19/month for unlimited transactions. See our pricing page for details.

Can I customize the receipt format?

Yes. You can customize receipt text, labels, and branding to match your organization's needs.
