A petty cash app fixes the loop by putting the record next to the cash — in the browser of whoever is actually handling it. Every transaction is logged the moment it happens, a numbered PDF receipt is generated for the handoff, and the running balance is visible to the owner from anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a petty cash app?

A petty cash app is a tool you use on your phone or browser to record every cash transaction in and out of a petty cash box. Instead of writing slips on paper or maintaining an Excel sheet, you log each entry in the app — amount, purpose, who took it, who received it — and the running balance updates automatically. SpendNote also generates a printable PDF receipt for every transaction, with a unique ID, so you have a documented record of the cash handoff.

Is there a free petty cash app?

SpendNote starts with a free 14-day trial, capped at 20 transactions (whichever comes first). It lets you track a single cash box with the basic receipt template and a basic PDF report — enough to test the full workflow on a real petty cash float. After the trial, continue on Standard or Pro from $15.83/month to unlock multi-box support, more team members, and unlimited transactions.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes — SpendNote runs in any modern browser on phone, tablet, or desktop, so the custodian can record a transaction the moment the cash leaves the box and the owner can watch the float from anywhere. There is nothing to install.

Does a petty cash app replace QuickBooks or accounting software?

No. A petty cash app handles the operational layer — who took cash, what it was for, what the float is right now, and the printable handoff receipt. Your accounting software still handles the formal journal entries and reconciliation against the GL. The petty cash app gives your bookkeeper a clean CSV or PDF export to work from at month-end.

Can multiple people use the same petty cash account?

Yes. The Pro plan includes role-based access so the custodian (the person who physically holds the cash), team members, and the owner each see what they need to. Every transaction is timestamped and tied to the user who recorded it.

What kind of receipt does the app produce?

Every transaction gets a unique ID (e.g. SN3-007) and a printable, Letter-size PDF receipt that documents the cash handoff. You can print it (two copies per page), download the PDF, or — on the Pro plan — email it directly from the transaction detail screen with a public PDF link for the recipient.

Can I customize the receipt layout?

Yes. On Standard and Pro you can upload your company logo, choose Quick or Detailed layout, and toggle which fields appear (addresses, tracking details, signatures). Pro adds full localization — customize every text label except the mandatory legal disclaimer.
