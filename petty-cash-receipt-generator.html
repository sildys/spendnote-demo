How the Petty Cash Receipt Generator Works

  1. Record transaction details - amount, date, handoff parties, and line items.
  2. Generate the receipt - SpendNote creates a professional cash receipt instantly.
  3. Deliver in your preferred format - print now, download PDF, or send by email.

Every generated receipt is saved with searchable history, so teams can trace who issued and who received cash without manual filing.
