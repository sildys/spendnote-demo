The Problem With Being On-Site

Paper-based petty cash only works when you're there. The log is in a drawer. The receipts are in an envelope. The balance is whatever the custodian says it is. If you're not on-site, you're blind.

This is fine when you're always present. But business owners travel, manage multiple locations, or work from home part of the week. The cash box doesn't pause when you leave — and that's where problems start.

Expenses get logged late (or not at all). Receipts pile up unreviewed. By the time you're back, the cash doesn't balance and nobody remembers why.

What Remote Petty Cash Management Looks Like

With a digital petty cash tracker, every transaction your team logs is visible to you instantly — from any device, anywhere. No phone calls asking "how much is left?" No waiting until Monday to review the week's spending.

Real-Time Balances

Every transaction updates the balance instantly. Open SpendNote in your phone’s browser and see the current state of every cash box — whether you're at home, on a trip, or at a different location.

Full Transaction History

Receipts by Email

Role-Based Access

When Remote Management Matters Most

  • Multiple locations — you can't be at every site. But you can see every site's cash box from one dashboard
  • Part-time presence — you're in the office 3 days a week. The cash box moves 5 days a week
  • Travel — you're on the road and your team needs to keep logging. You review when you land
  • Delegation — you've delegated petty cash to a staff member but still need visibility

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I track petty cash from my phone?

Yes. SpendNote is a web app that works in any browser — on your phone, tablet, or computer. You can check balances, review transactions, and even receive receipts by email without being at the office.

Do I need to be on-site to see the cash box balance?

No. Every transaction logged by your team updates the balance in real time. You can open SpendNote from anywhere and see the current balance, recent transactions, and who recorded them — without touching the physical cash box.

What if my staff logs transactions while I'm away?

Every transaction is timestamped and attributed to the user who logged it. You can review the full history when you're back, or check in remotely at any time. If something looks wrong, the transaction detail shows exactly who entered it and when.

Is remote petty cash management secure?

SpendNote uses role-based access control. You decide who can view, add transactions, or manage settings for each cash box. Users can only see the cash boxes they're assigned to. The owner retains full control regardless of location.
